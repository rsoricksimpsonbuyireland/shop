Shipping Policy

Free shipments for orders of €199 or more!

You can choose between a standard shipping method (3-21 days) and the UPS delivery (UPS is often faster). However, UPS is only possible for CBD products at the moment. RSO products can only be dispatched with the standard post which takes about a week (3-5 working days), but can take longer due to increased border security.

Order Processing, Delivery Method, and Timing: After an order has been received in our system, a processing time of 1 to 3 business days is required to review the order. Our RSO and CBD house products are sent using a Dutch Post Company as a registered package that needs a signature upon delivery. Shipping times is dependent on location from our main warehouse located in the Netherlands. Typical shipping times may vary from 2 to 7 business days (Monday to Friday) after processing times, this does not include any potential for inclement weather, holidays, or backorders. If a backorder takes place, we will do our best to notify you about the backorder and provide the estimated next shipment date. Orders marked completed will be given tracking information in a form of an email that is sent automatically to you.
